About

Our goal is to build a LoRa-based data network with complete, redundant coverage of Tompkins County, as part of The Things Network.

The network is free to access, is based on open source software, and is owned and operated by its users.

If you are interested in getting involved, please join our Meetup group, and register on our community page on the global site..
